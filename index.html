Plan Your Night Out

Inspired by "The World's End" film written by Wright and Simon Pegg, this app is designed to help you plan your own "Golden Mile" in your hometown or desired location.

Simply enter your city to search for the most popular bars, pubs and breweries nearest your location according to FourSquare. Filter through your favourite locations and add them to your list (up to 8 locations) to create your crawl for the night!
